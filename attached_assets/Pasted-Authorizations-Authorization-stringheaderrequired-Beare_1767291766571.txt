Authorizations

Authorization
stringheaderrequired
Bearer authentication header of the form Bearer <token>, where <token> is your auth token.

enum<string>required

"2FA"

stringrequired
Human-readable summary of the desired use-case

stringrequired

"START, YES, SUBSCRIBE"
